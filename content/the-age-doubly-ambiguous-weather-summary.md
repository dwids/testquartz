The top left hand corner of [The Age](http://www.theage.com.au/) website has a little Weather section, which you can personalise.

Here&#8217;s mine for just now:
![Age Weather Image](http://members.iinet.net.au/~dsidwell1/luthien/Age_weatherInfo.jpg)

If you glance at it, two things jump out at you:

1. The term **change**. That&#8217;s a weather term, usually meaning cool change. Here it means change your city
2. The word **Snow **. Besides* Full Details*.  Sunny then Snow? Unfortunate layout as it&#8217;s actually *Snow Report*, with the 2nd word on the next line&#8230;

I&#8217;ve actually reported the first one to them already and they thanked me.  Only spotted the second one today.
