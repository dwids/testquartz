Captured live at The Espy pub during episodes of the much-missed *RockWiz*  on SBS. Every episode of this brilliant music-quiz show, finished with a duet. It was with artists who may have never worked together and possibly from different genres than they usually work in.

### Sarah McLeod & Angry Anderson – Highway To Hell

On the duets compilation DVD, the superb house-band said how much they loved doing this and really got into it. And doesn’t it show. I love her t-shirt.

### Chris Cheney & Chrissy Amphlett – Stray Cat Blues

The late Chrissy in full flight. With her own sweet take on the lyrics. And Chris..and the band…and everything. Magical Rolling Stones cover. RIP Chrissy

### Troy Cassar-Daly & Ella Hooper – Islands In The Stream

A guilty pleasure of mine ; I’ve always loved this song. I also love the way that Ella seems to be looking down to see the lyrics. Not her usual style but a brilliant, joyful pairing.
