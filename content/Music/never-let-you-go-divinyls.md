From the first time I heard their debut single &#8211; Boys in Town &#8211; I loved The Divinyls.  Rare for a male voice to be heard on one of their songs, as there&#8217;s usually a female lead with her fantastic vocals.  But this one starts with a bloke before Christine Joy Amphlett rips in.  Not only that, but for those learning English there&#8217;s an alphabetical-treat, just for you. From 1983, this song is also known as &#8220;Siren (Never Let You Go)&#8221;. No matter what it&#8217;s called, it&#8217;s just so good:
