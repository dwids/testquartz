A 2nd Australian anti-war song, again with a subtle message, like  [yesterdays](rachel-russell-morris.md) From 1969, this is more obviously commenting on the Vietnam war.  It was, I read, written by Johnny Young (yes, of Young Talent Time) about his mate and fellow singer, Normie Rowe.  Rowe had been sent &#8220;off to the Asian War&#8220;.  To be fair, some sources say &#8220;[inducted](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Normie_Rowe#National_Service:_1968.E2.80.931972)&#8221; others say &#8220;[drafted](http://www.heraldsun.com.au/news/national/new-twist-on-normie-rowe-draft-claim/story-e6frf7l6-1111116407353)&#8220;.  So I&#8217;m not 100% sure.  But here&#8217;s Ronnie Burns with this heartfelt song.
