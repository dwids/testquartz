Text

Captured live at The Espy pub during episodes of the much-missed *RockWiz*. Every episode of the show finished with a duet, including with artists who may have never worked together and possibly from different genres.  

### Sarah McLeod & Angry Anderson – Highway To Hell

On the duets compilation DVD, the superb house-band said how much they loved doing this and really go into it. And doesn’t it show. I love her t-shirt.

### Chris Cheney & Chrissy Amphlett – Stray Cat Blues

The late Chr[i](https://youtu.be/WnOXYcO4zjg?ref=davidphoto.org)ssy in full flight. With her own sweet take on the lyrics. And Chris..and the band…and everything. Magical Rolling Stones cover. RIP Chr[i](https://youtu.be/WnOXYcO4zjg?ref=davidphoto.org)ssy. 

### Troy Cassar-Daly & Ella Hooper – Islands In The Stream

A guilty pleasure of mine ; I’ve always loved this song. I also love the way that Ell[a](https://youtu.be/01aq2JWk3d0?ref=davidphoto.org) seems to be looking down to see the lyrics. Not her usual style but a brilliant, joyful pairing.

### With a little help from my friends – Dan Sultan & Ella Hooper

Superb. That’s all I have to say.

### Tina Arena & Jeff Martin – Don’t Give Up

Stunning and beautiful and amazing.
