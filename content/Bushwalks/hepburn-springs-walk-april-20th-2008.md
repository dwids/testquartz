Another pleasant walk from[ Daywalks Around Melbourne](__GHOST_URL__/?p=146) ; walk 42 &#8211; Hunts Dam and Argyle Spring. Did this as a day trip; drove to Hepburn Springs, did the walk and drove home. Not problems at all.

Not a real &#8216;gee whiz&#8217; walk and rated medium/difficult not because of terrain or steepness, but navigation. A few tracks don&#8217;t have names so instructions say things like about 1 km along turn left into un-named side track.  I confess to getting a bit lost, but found my way back on track again.   As I said, pleasant and worth the slight challenges of the navigation.
