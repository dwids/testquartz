I was asked where some photos I had (in my Apple TV screensaver) were taken.  Most were from the same trip, it turns out. Down the South West of Victoria.

## Photo 1

[![image](8661a-image_thumb.png)](https://dwids.wordpress.com2020/03/37cb0-image.png)

This is Aire River near the **Aire River West Campground**. [More info here.](http://parkweb.vic.gov.au/explore/parks/great-otway-national-park/things-to-do/aire-river-west2)  It’s between Cape Otway and the 12 Apostles. Even more precise it’s between Cape Otway and Johanna Beach.

## Photos 2 & 3

[![image](8ab8b-image_thumb1.png)](https://dwids.wordpress.com2020/03/f05d0-image1.png)[![image](ccf04-image_thumb2.png)](https://dwids.wordpress.com2020/03/3ad55-image2.png)

Johanna River as it enters the wild sea at **Johanna Beach**. Also the scary waves at Johanna Beach. The River is a short walk (South east) from the car park at the Johanna Beach camping area.  Between Aire River and 12 Apostles area.

## Photo 4

[![image](0074b-image_thumb3.png)](https://dwids.wordpress.com2020/03/e46c7-image3.png)
**Northern Grampians**.  Near the Mt Zero Log Cabins, looking south towards Mt Zero and Mt Stapylton.

## Photo 5

[![image](d1403-image_thumb4.png)](https://dwids.wordpress.com2020/03/9c2c5-image4.png)

The other end of the Grampians. Coming East from Hamilton area towards Dunkeld in the **Southern Grampians**. One of those is Mt Abrupt, I think.
