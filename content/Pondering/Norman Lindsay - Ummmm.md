## Aprils Fools Day and Me  

### As an Adult

Only once, as an adult, did a prank take me in. An FM radio station had a news story about how ***we had decoded dolphin's squeaks and clicks***! In other words; their language.  It was all very rational, from memory, and it was two-way.  They even had a simple 'grammar'  *D'Oh!*

### When I was Younger

A few, TV ones, got me in . Again, they all appealed to my science.maths.logical brain:

#### Spaghetti

The ***Spaghetti Harvest in Victoria had failed***!  It showed people picking it off shrubs. I went "oh no!"  

#### Time
Not long after we went metric in the 1970s, it was announced that ***Time was Going Metric***! They even had a clockmaker make a physical "10 metric hour" clock. I was worried about how we could convert 24 evenly into 10.

#### Westgate
The Westgate Bridge was built from 2 ends; which met the middle. Sometime before it was finished, it was announced at ***The Two Halves were Misaligned!***.  And there would need to be right angle turn in the middle. They even had a proper drawing of the new layout.
