## About this SiC blog.

*Ancora imparo.*

*I am still learning. *

That is the motto of Monash University, where I studied back when. And I&#8217;ve openly adopted it for myself.

I like to learn new things and then, if I find them sufficiently interesting, write them up here.  Or take things I already know and write them up.  In most cases for a &#8216;general&#8217; audience. And on a wide range of topics:

Movies, TV shows, podcasts, eBooks, music appreciation (interesting songs), music theory, how to play the piano, photography, Internet (WiFi, phones, underlying networking technologies),  programming,  hiking and navigation, photography and video-editing. And so on.

To be honest, it&#8217;s not a &#8216;*Please give me Comments*; or *Like and Subscribe&#8217;* blog. If I was that needy and insecure, I&#8217;d be back on Facebook 🙂  

But seriously, it&#8217;s more of a*** Journal of Things I Find Interesting And You May Too.  ***

It&#8217;s a split satisfaction model; I&#8217;m happy to write it and just happy to know that some of you read it and might learn something. Still. 

&#8211;  David

(Pseudo-intellectual & Semi-polymath)
