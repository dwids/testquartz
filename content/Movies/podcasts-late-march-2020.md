## Music and Movies

I discovered podcasts very early. Great for listening to on walks, drives or even doing the chores.  Here&#8217;s one&#8217;s I&#8217;ve historically enjoyed or am currently listening too. Just a subset. 

Search for these in your favourite podcast software. On Android I use the paid version of **BeyondPod.**

### You Must Remember This

&#8220;This is a storytelling podcast exploring the secret and/or forgotten histories of Hollywood&#8217;s first century. It&#8217;s the brainchild and passion project of Karina Longworth (founder of Cinematical.com, former film critic for LA Weekly), who writes, narrates, records and edits each episode&#8221; 

The Manson series is particularly good. But so is all her stuff.

https://www.youmustrememberthispodcast.com/

### Disgraceland

&#8220;Disgraceland is a podcast about musicians getting away with murder and behaving very badly. Thirty-ish minute episodes that trace the most insane criminal stories surrounding our most interesting and infamous pop stars.&#8221;

https://www.disgracelandpod.com/

### 27 Club

Stories about musical icons who died at the age of 27. This first season is on the life and times of Jimi Hendrix

https://27clubpod.com/

### Strong Songs

&#8220;Music: it&#8217;s good. On each episode of Strong Songs, host Kirk Hamilton takes listeners inside a piece of music, breaking it down and figuring out what makes it work.&#8221;

This is just wonderful, if you like a deeper look at specific songs and why they &#8216;work&#8217;.  

He not only explains how the (drums, vocals, guitar, french horn, harmonies, piano, drums&#8230;) do their bit, he plays that bit for you.  Then he&#8217;ll layer in the other elements- yes, playing them all &#8211; to show you how the song is built up.

*Kiss From a Rose,  Like A Prayer, God Only Know, Stairway to Heaven*&#8230;

http://strongsongspodcast.com/
