One of those, hey why didn&#8217;t I think of that ideas.

Your hand-held camcorder/phone etc video is always going to wobble. So the subject moves around within the fixed frame.

So why not use software (like Motion 3 on your mac)  to process each frame and make the subject still and move the frame around it. As per [this](http://www.flickr.com/photos/ibftp/2425396334/in/photostream/)[http://www.flickr.com/photos/ibftp/2425396334/in/photostream/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/ibftp/2425396334/in/photostream/). Then you can make [nice videos](http://www.flickr.com/photos/ibftp/2425386864/in/set-72157604484198185/). Which also has a link to the stabilised Zapruder film too (obviously a disturbing video, so be careful)

Original article at [LifeHacker](http://www.lifehacker.com.au/tips/2008/06/14/_make_your_own_stabilized_video_collage_-2.html).
