I&#8217;ve been using Spotify for some time and had seen that when you search for an Artist or Band, you get **their **&#8220;*This is*..&#8221; and &#8220;..*Radio*&#8220;.  Like this, for Van Morrison:
![](4_image.png)
*This is Van Morrison* is just his &#8216;essential tracks&#8217; in one playlist.  Not his greatest hits, nor most popular. But anyway, not the point here 🙂

*Van Morrison Radio* is another playlist that usually starts with one of his songs, but then has **similar artist&#8217;s****tracks**, then one of Van&#8217;s, then similar artist&#8217;s etc. 

Now, I was only told of the** Song Radio** last week. It generates a list of **similar songs** from a range of artists. It&#8217;s very good. You select a **song **and from the pop up menu (click on the three dots &#8230;) then click on *Go to Song Radio*. 

I love Peter Gabriel&#8217;s *Solsbury Hill*, so here we go:
![](2_image-1.png)
And we get this great *Solsbury Hill Radio* playlist of **similar songs**.
![](ss.20200320.120228.png)
