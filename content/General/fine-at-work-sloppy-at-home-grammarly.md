So I&#8217;m trying out the free version of Grammarly and the associated plugin for Chrome. It doesn&#8217;t play quite nicely with the WordPress &#8216;block editor&#8217; (*Gutenberg*) but there are easy workarounds.

It&#8217;s pretty good at spotting obvious errors. And giving me valuable feedback on how I&#8217;m doing!
