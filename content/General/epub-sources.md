# ePub Sources

## Standard Ebooks  (US)

[https://standardebooks.org/](https://standardebooks.org/)  "...a volunteer-driven project that produces new editions of public domain ebooks that are lovingly formatted, open source, free of U.S. copyright restrictions, and free of cost."

## Faded Page (Canada)

[https://www.fadedpage.com/index.php](https://www.fadedpage.com/index.php) "These books are public domain in Canada (because we follow the Canadian copyright laws),"    Distributed Proofreaders Canada (DPC) publish here (and also some to Gutenberg etc)

## Global Grey (UK)

[https://www.globalgreyebooks.com/index.html](https://www.globalgreyebooks.com/index.html)

"My name's Julie and I've been running this site for around twelve years. I format all the ebooks myself, as well as maintaining the website. This is an ongoing project that was born from a love of reading and a commitment to make free ebooks that look good."

## Project Gutenberg Australia

[https://gutenberg.net.au/](https://gutenberg.net.au/)

## Roy Glashan's Library (Australia)

[https://freeread.com.au/](https://freeread.com.au/)

"This library gives readers access to a selection of free e-books hosted at two web sites—Roy Glashan's Library (RGL, formerly Freeread) and Project Gutenberg Australia (PGA)"
