Decided to read my first ever Harry Potter book, having seen every film thus far. Given the Half Blood Prince movie is due out in July, [the book of the same name](http://www.amazon.com/Harry-Potter-Half-Blood-Prince-Book/dp/0439785960/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;s=books&amp;qid=1244959326&amp;sr=1-1) was the obvious choice.&#160; And I loved it.

Even though it weighs in at 600+ pages, JKR writes so well that you hardly notice the length.&#160; As Harry gets older, so does his audience, so the tone of the book is very dark.&#160; The final trailer for the movie came out just as I finished and it too is dark.&#160;
